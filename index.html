About

I'm an embedded systems engineer wrapping up my Master's at UMD (GPA 3.95). I live in that sweet spot where firmware meets silicon, bare-metal C on STM32s one day, FPGA accelerators in Verilog the next. I've worked on GNSS hardware at a space center, MRI coils actuation on Zynq FPGAs at a medical startup, and audio compression with neural networks for Dolby. When I'm not debugging timing issues, I'm probably soldering something I shouldn't be, been at it since age 7.
